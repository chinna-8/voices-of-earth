Climate Change Resources

Below are a variety of resources to help you learn more about climate change, take action, and get involved in the fight to protect our planet.

Educational Resources

  • Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) - The IPCC provides the most comprehensive scientific reports on climate change, its effects, and potential solutions.
  • NASA Climate Change and Global Warming - An extensive resource from NASA with scientific data, research, and evidence about global warming and its effects.
  • National Geographic - Climate Change - A collection of articles, reports, and videos covering the science, effects, and solutions to climate change.
  • Climate.gov - A resource from NOAA that offers science, education materials, and tools related to climate change and its impacts.
  • 350.org - A global movement dedicated to reducing CO2 levels to 350 ppm (parts per million) to restore a safe climate.

Take Action: Get Involved

  • Earth Day Network - Earth Day is a worldwide event on April 22 every year to raise awareness about environmental issues and promote action on climate change.
  • Greenpeace USA - Greenpeace works on issues like climate change, deforestation, and pollution, empowering people to take action for a greener future.
  • Sierra Club - An American environmental organization focused on protecting the environment and advancing environmental justice.
  • World Wildlife Fund (WWF) - WWF works globally to protect the world's most vulnerable ecosystems and wildlife from climate change impacts.
  • Citizens' Climate Lobby - A grassroots organization working to build political will for solutions to climate change, such as carbon pricing.

Tools and Resources for Reducing Your Carbon Footprint

  • Carbon Footprint Calculator - A tool to estimate your personal or household carbon footprint and learn ways to reduce it.
  • One Earth - A platform providing climate-conscious lifestyle tips, as well as information on how to reduce emissions through everyday actions.
  • Cool Effect - Cool Effect offers tools to calculate and reduce your carbon footprint through high-impact carbon reduction projects.
  • Ecosia - A search engine that uses its profits to plant trees, helping mitigate deforestation and combat climate change.

Climate Change News and Updates

  • The Guardian - Climate Crisis - A collection of the latest articles and news on the global climate crisis from The Guardian.
  • BBC - Science and Environment - A section on BBC News dedicated to climate change and related environmental news.
  • Reuters - Environment News - News and stories on climate change, environmental sustainability, and renewable energy.

Books to Read on Climate Change

  • "The Uninhabitable Earth" by David Wallace-Wells - A powerful exploration of the potential impacts of climate change on human civilization.
  • "This Changes Everything" by Naomi Klein - A compelling argument that climate change is not just an environmental issue, but a political, economic, and moral one.
  • "The Sixth Extinction" by Elizabeth Kolbert - A Pulitzer Prize-winning book exploring the ongoing mass extinction event caused by human actions, including climate change.
  • "The Hidden Life of Trees" by Peter Wohlleben - A fascinating look at how trees interact with each other and the world around them, and their role in combating climate change.
